Asteroids

Destroy all the asteroids, while avoiding them to win the game, and get the highest score possible.
Shooting at big asteroid will cause it to split into 2 small asteroids, and you will gain 5 points.
Shooting at small asteroid will cause it to be destroyed and you will gain 10 points.

Move: key w
Turn Left: key a
Turn Right: key d
Shoot: key SPACE
